FINE DINING AT UNMESHA RESTAURANT

"Step into the opulent world of Unmesha's Spiceroutes, where the rich flavors of Kerala cuisine meet the bold twists of fusion dining.
Our luxurious restaurant transports you to the majestic landscapes of God's Own Country, with intricate wooden carvings, lush greenery, and warm golden lighting.
Savor our signature dishes, such as the 'Kerala Chicken Tikka Masala' and 'Idiyappam Wrapped Grilled Shrimp', crafted by our expert chefs who blend traditional spices with modern flair.
As you indulge in our exquisite Kerala-Fusion cuisine, paired with an extensive selection of fine wines and cocktails, you'll experience the true essence of luxury dining in a warm and inviting ambiance."
